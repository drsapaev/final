# Landing Conversion

Audit whether the page deserves the conversion it asks for. Save findings to `audits/conversion-audit.json`.

## Review Areas

- promise strength
- proof and trust placement
- CTA timing and repetition
- objection handling
- risk reversal
- friction between interest and action

## Guardrails

- conversion advice must respect the actual offer and audience
- do not suggest dark patterns or misleading urgency
