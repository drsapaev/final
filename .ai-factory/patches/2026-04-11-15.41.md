# Billing Payment Semantics Fix

## Summary
`Appointment.visit_type = "paid"` now remains a visit/service type and is no longer treated as proof that payment has been completed.

## Changed
- Updated `backend/app/services/billing_service.py`.
- `is_appointment_paid()` now ignores `visit_type="paid"` as payment evidence.
- Removed generic `Appointment.status in ["paid", "completed", "done"]` payment inference.
- Payment completion is now based on explicit payment markers or persisted `Payment` records.
- `update_appointment_payment_status()` sets `payment_processed_at` instead of rewriting `visit_type`.
