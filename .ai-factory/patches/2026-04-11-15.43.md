# Registrar Wizard Payment Read Model Fix

## Summary
Registrar wizard appointment read-model no longer treats `visit_type="paid"` or operational statuses as proof that payment is completed.

## Changed
- Updated `backend/app/api/v1/endpoints/registrar_wizard.py`.
- Appointment `payment_status` now comes from `payment_processed_at`, linked visit `discount_mode="paid"`, linked visit payment marker, or persisted `Payment` rows.
- Removed GET-side mutation that rewrote `Appointment.visit_type` to `"paid"`.
- Visit `payment_status` now comes from `discount_mode="paid"`, `payment_processed_at`, or persisted `Payment` rows, not from `Visit.status`.
- Legacy `status="paid"` values are normalized to operational `waiting` in the registrar wizard read-model response.
