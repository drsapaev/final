# Registrar Integration Payment-State Separation

## Summary
Starting a queue visit from registrar integration no longer creates a paid payment or rewrites appointment `visit_type` from operational movement.

## Changed
- Updated `backend/app/api/v1/endpoints/registrar_integration.py`.
- `start_queue_visit` still changes operational status to `in_progress`.
- Existing paid payments can still hydrate `Visit.discount_mode="paid"`.
- Removed automatic paid payment creation based only on `in_progress`.
- Removed `Appointment.visit_type="paid"` writes based on payment amount or operational status.
