# Cashier Endpoint Payment-State Separation

## Summary
Cashier payment actions no longer write `Visit.status="paid"` as a payment marker.

## Changed
- Updated `backend/app/api/v1/endpoints/cashier.py`.
- Added a small helper that preserves operational visit status and maps legacy `paid` to `waiting`.
- `mark_visit_as_paid()` now creates/marks payment and sets `discount_mode="paid"` without overwriting `Visit.status`.
- `confirm_payment()` now sets only the payment marker on the visit.
- `cancel_payment()` and full refund now clear `discount_mode` without forcing an unrelated status transition.
